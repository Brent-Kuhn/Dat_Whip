### FLAGS ###
### Changing these change major functions of the car ###

DISPLAY_IMAGE: Whether to display images or not (should be FALSE when not plugged in to a TV)

STREAM_IMAGE: Whether to send images over network (leave FALSE unless specifically debugging)

### CV CONFIGURATIONS ###
### Configure these to modify how the line is processed ###

GAUSS_KERNEL: Size of the gaussian blur radius

CANNY_LOW_THRESH: Canny edge detection parameters
CANNY_HIGH_THRESH

REGION_OF_INTEREST_P1_X: Region to crop line-finding image, as percentages of the image size
REGION_OF_INTEREST_P1_Y
REGION_OF_INTEREST_P2_X
REGION_OF_INTEREST_P2_Y
REGION_OF_INTEREST_P3_X
REGION_OF_INTEREST_P3_Y
REGION_OF_INTEREST_P4_X
REGION_OF_INTEREST_P4_Y

HOUGH_RHO: Hough line parameters... I don't honestly understand what these do
HOUGH_THETA_DENOM
HOUGH_THRESH
HOUGH_MIN_LEN
HOUGH_MAX_GAP

BLUE_HUE: Define the exact blue color we are looking for for line following
BLUE_HUE_THRESH
BLUE_SAT_MIN
BLUE_SAT_MAX
BLUE_VAL_MIN
BLUE_VAL_MAX

### LINE PROCESSING ###
### Change to modify how the hough lines are condensed to a single line ###

SLOPE_MIN_THRESH: Minimum slope, any lines underneath this slope will be ignored

HISTORY_SIZE: How many previous lines to keep for the moving average
